Course Summary

This online movie production course will teach you about the fundamentals of how movies are made and produced. In this film production course, study a wide range of topics in filmmaking process, including theme, genre, narrative structure, story, directing, acting, cinematography, photography, shooting, editing, and sound. Learn about the basic principles of character development, how to create a three-dimensional character, and a lot more!
